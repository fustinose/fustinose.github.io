TalkMeUp Inc.

Backend Engineer Intern

I had my first internship as a software engineer at TalkMeUp Inc., which was a startup company located in Pittsburgh. It was the first time I got exposed to real industry software development. We used Node.js and React to build our web-based product. My main responsibility surrounded our database. I led the design and implementation of our MongoDB collections to serve our frontend requests. Specifically,

  • I designed and developed the majority of the database collections for our online training courses.
  • I proposed and implemented an inverted index for one of the collections, which proved important for metric analysis.
  • I designed and implemented various database query APIs, including connection with AI service module, data aggregation, and regression analysis. I also optimized many I/O code and achieved 5x speedup.
