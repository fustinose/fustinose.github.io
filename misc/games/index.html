My Life with Video Games

I played my first video game when I was about 4 years old. It was on my dad’s old GameBoy, and I believe it was a street fighter game. When PSP first came out, my dad bought me one, and since then my life was flooded with video games.

I later (around middle school) came to know Dota2, and through it I gradually switched to computer games (good job Gabe Newell). I would not call myself an expert player, but I think I am not a bad one.

What I Play

Now I play mostly action games. I have played most of the Steam hits one may heard of. My favorites are

  • Nioh (all time favorite)
  • Monster Hunter
  • Dark Souls, Sekiro, (Elden Ring), anything FromSoftware
